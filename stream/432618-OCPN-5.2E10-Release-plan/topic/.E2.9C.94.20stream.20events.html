Pavel Kalian (Apr 06 2024 at 15:44):

No, two different things. The cached tiles are for bitmap tiles, satellite photos etc.
The basemap is built on https://osmdata.openstreetmap.de/data/land-polygons.html land polygon dataset. We need it to be vector for the land crossing functionality. And shapefiles as technology, because going forward we actually need generic shapefile support as there are many interesting datasets available in this format.
