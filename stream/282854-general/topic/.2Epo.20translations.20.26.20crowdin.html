Pavel Kalian (Mar 10 2026 at 12:21):

You need to build the sar-pot-update target (Eg. using make sar-pot-update on Unix systems. You will find out that po/SAR_PI.potfile is named wrong and needs to be renamed all lowercase for the stuff to actually work on case sensitive filesystems).
Then that file needs to be uploaded to Crowdin (If you don't have privileges for that, let me or @Håkan Svensson know and we will do it for you)
Once you want the new .po files with translations, just download them from Crowdin (Build & Download button at https://crowdin.com/project/opencpn/translations - Again ask me or Hakan if you need help with that), copy yours to the po directory and commit them to the plugin sources.
