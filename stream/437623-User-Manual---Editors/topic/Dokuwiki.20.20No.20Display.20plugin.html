Rick Gleason (Jun 05 2024 at 11:24):

I would like to bring to your attention, this useful plugin.
We can have a conversation or make comments within the OpenCPN wiki that are only visible to admins and managers. Managers are "Editors" just use the nodisplay syntax

<nodisp> your text here </nodisp>

<nodisp>
your text here
etc
</nodisp>

See https://www.dokuwiki.org/plugin:nodisp
We should probably start using this now.
