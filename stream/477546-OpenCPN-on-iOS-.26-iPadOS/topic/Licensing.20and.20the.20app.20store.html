Topic: Licensing and the app store

Tony Voss (Jan 09 2025 at 13:02):

It is said that the Apple app store is fundamentally incompatible with the open source licenses that come with wxWidgets and our own code. However, releasing apps via the apple app store is not the only way of distributing an app.

For users in the EU there is, for starters, the Alt Store. Basically, you can install an app Altstore PAL which allows you to download apps from the Alt Store. This can include 'self-published' apps. Updating of apps is supported

For users outside the EU, Alt Store provides an app for MacOS that allows side loading to an iOS/iPadOS device. So you need a MacOS system to which you download the app and then load into the device.
